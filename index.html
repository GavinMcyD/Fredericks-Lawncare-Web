About Us

Fredericks Lawncare is a locally owned, family run lawn care and snow removal service proudly serving Terre Haute and the surrounding areas since 2016. Founded by Cameron at just 16 years old with a mower and a strong work ethic, the business grew through word of mouth and a commitment to dependable, high quality service. As demand increased, his brother Cole joined the team, and today the two brothers run Fredericks Lawncare together, combining years of hands-on experience with a shared dedication to customer satisfaction. From routine mowing and trimming to edging, seasonal cleanups, snow removal, and general property maintenance, we focus on keeping every home and business neat, safe, and looking its best year-round. Our goal is simple: show up on time, communicate clearly, and treat every property with the same care and attention we would give our own.
