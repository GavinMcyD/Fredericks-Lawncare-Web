About Us

Fredericks Lawncare is a locally owned and operated lawn service proudly serving Terre Haute and the surrounding communities since 2016. What started as a small neighborhood mowing service has grown into a trusted lawn care business known for reliability, quality work, and attention to detail.

The business was founded by Cameron when he was just 16 years old. With a mower, a strong work ethic, and a goal to provide dependable service, he began helping neighbors keep their yards clean and well maintained. As demand grew, so did the business built on word of mouth, repeat customers, and a commitment to doing the job right.

A few years later, his brother Cole joined the team, bringing the same dedication and pride in quality work. Today, the two brothers run Fredericks Lawncare together, combining their experience and hands-on approach to deliver professional results while maintaining the personal touch of a family run business.

We specialize in routine lawn mowing, trimming, edging, seasonal cleanups, and general yard maintenance. Our goal is simple: to keep your property looking its best while providing dependable, friendly service you can count on.

At Fredericks Lawncare, we believe in showing up on time, communicating clearly, and treating every yard with the same care and respect we would give our own. We are proud to serve the Terre Haute community and look forward to helping you maintain a clean, healthy, and great-looking lawn.
